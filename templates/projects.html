Project Overview

There might be two different texts, which cover same subjects. Danchew helps user to recognize the different viewpoint existing between two texts. By comparing two texts, an user can have more balanced viewpoint in many issues, including social and global issues.

When an user enters two texts in the form and submit, Danchew shows 3 different tag-cloud. First and second tag-cloud represent morphemes exsisting in each text, and third tag-cloud displays common-morphemes, existing in both of them. So an user can understand overall content from the third one, and different viewpoint from first and second.

Role

Ideation, Importing Facebook and Twitter API, Making lists of 'Most recent articles' and 'Top viewed articles' from stored dataset.

Project Overview

The purpose of this study is to introduce a new way to express schedulers.

We recognized that almost all schedulers are rectangular in shape and we wanted to change this shape using a novel way of expression. We thought that daily, weekly, and monthly schedules came to us regularly and the movement of these schedules is like a planet’s revolution. Therefore, we developed a tool that converts users’ Google Calendar into a planetary system.

After applying one's Google Calendar by clicking a button on the top, users could see their regular and irregular schedules are coming toward the 'Today' line. Each schedule is represented as a planet with a color and this color is same as the color of schedules in Google Calendar. The number of planets shows the number of schedules in one day. Regular schedules are displayed on the right side and irregular schedules are on the other side. Users could see the movement of the schedules by moving a sidebar displayed on the bottom.

Role

Ideation, Designing main orbits, Implementing the movements and positions of planets.

Project Overview

The purpose of this study is to introduce the Sawtooth Bubble Chart as a way to visualize the human emotional data.

According to James Russell, human emotions can be explained by two constructs: valence level (from pleasant to unpleasant) and arousal level (from activation to deactivation). This study utilized the bubble charts to present those two constructs. To indicate various human emotions, the levels of valence were described by sawtooth on each bubble; the levels of arousal were described by different colors.

As the number of users of Social Networking Service rises, the demands for visualizing human emotions are increasing. Before this study, most of visualization methods were based on unidimensional classification of human emotions. However, extracting detailed human emotions are getting easier thanks to the development of the text mining algorithm. Therefore, this study proposes new visualization methodology which can express multidimensional human emotions more effectively. In future, studies for finding more applications to verify the effectiveness of this method are needed.

Role

Ideation, Analyzing Twitter data and ANEW dictionary, Writing a paper

Project Overview

This project is about analyzing Korean texts based on the Vector Methodology.

In this project, due to the inherent characteristic of Korean morphemes and grammar, it’s hard to directly apply text analysis methodology in English text. So we devise a system based on vector analysis. Each element in a N-dimension vector represents the number of advents in N-th sentence. Therefore, if we have 5 sentences and the word “is” appears in 1st, 3rd and 4th sentence, it’s vector representation is (1, 0, 1, 1, 0). In this way, if we calculate cosine value from the inner product of two vectors, that value can represent correlation of two words. If two words have higher cosine value than usual pairs of words, then we can put these words nearby.

Some of the words in a text are regarded as significant than others, based on the position in a paragraph. Since important words tent to appear in the first or the last sentence of a paragraph. We put more weight on those words to calculate “importance factor”. In visualizations, we can use “correlation value” to determine the relative position of a morpheme and “importance factor” to determine the size of a morpheme.

Role

Ideation, Modifying morpheme analyzing toolkit, Implementing vector analysis, Visualizing results with D3.js Heatmap

Project Overview

This project is purposed to introduce Arduino to junior-high school students.

As a selected volunteer program supported by Seoul National University Institute for Global Social Responsibility, Our team made advertising materials in order to gather participants. We decided our target as junior-high students because we thought they could not only understand computer programming methodologies well, but also enjoy them. About 15 students from Yeoksam Middle School participated.

We prepared lecture materials with simple example codes and circuits in order to make students understand both computer programming and Microcontroller. Then, based on what they had learned, students proposed their own projects which were related to make simple gadgets using Arduino. Our team member helped them to complete their projects.

Role

Writing a proposal, Making education materials, Teaching students
